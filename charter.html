Invstme is a company that exists for the public benefit in addition to the traditional corporate goal of maximizing profit for shareholders.
Our Charter
We believe in a set of ideals: democracy, rights, liberty, opportunity and equality. These are seen and exemplified throughout this site. Invstme want's to protect and uphold the idea of "The American Dream." At Invstme, out goal is to strengthen the economic growth while simultaneously decreasing the disparity of wealth. We intend on acomplishing this by sharing the wealth from innovation with all who were involved.

  1. Small & Lean Startups - Small companies that are efficient and innovative are able to quickly move forward to accomplish their goals. This leads to an increase of wealth in the economy.
  2. Wisdom of The Crowd - The collective opinion of a group of individuals is far better than a single expert. This is a big aspect in Invstme where we circumvent the traditional gatekeepers of funding that exist for entrepreneurs.
  3. Investor Equality - Everyone has a right to invest in what they believe in and are passionate about. This applies especially to individuals who have the means to invest but simply do not fulfil the criteria of a high net individual.
  4. Investing In The Future - Investing in new and innovative ideas positively affects everyone regardless of it’s success or failure. One of the hardest things to accomplish is positive change in the world. This is why most startups fail. There are so many moving gears that all have to synchronize in unison. Invstme attempts to simplify one of these gears so that it’s one less thing founders must focus on, leaving more time for accomplishing the more complex aspects of a business.
Invstme's Promises & Obligations 

  • 5% of Invstme's Total Profits - We will donate 5% of our total profits every year. At Invstme, we believe in practicing what we preach. Therefore we will be funding startups, providing training and connecting mentors with founders. Our goal is to assist in the creation of new and innovative companies not only in Canada but all over the world.
  • The Truest Form of Democracy - We believe that everyone, not just the wealthy and powerful should have the ability to invest in companies. Anyone with the means to invest in companies should have the ability to invest their money where they wish to.
  • Simplify Finance - Invstme is all about plain language. We insist our founders and investors both simplify their language in their dealings on the platform.
  • Fund Ideas So Crazy, They Just Might Work - We love non-traditional ideas that sound ridiculous, technically challenging and ahead of their time. We want to help everyone invest in the future. Ideas that capture out imagination range from space travel to medical research and everything in-between. 
  • Transparency - Invstme will educate both investors and entrepreneurs to provide clear and consise information both ways. By founders and investors working together we intend on streamlining the process of innovation and wealth building.
