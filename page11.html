Invstme

Kickstarter meets the stock market - a revolution in startup funding. 

Invstme was a platform built out of necessity. Throughout our journey as entrepreneurs we met a number of bright & motivated individuals with brilliant ideas. Unfortunately, they were unable to raise the necessary funding to start because of the risk attached with startups. At Invstme we applaud those who have ideas and are willing to help shape the world into a better place for all. In a world that desperately needs innovation, we believe that ideas “out of the box” are our best hope.
Similarly to the entrepreneur side, we didn’t understand why everyday individuals couldn’t invest in businesses just because they lacked the necessary wealth. The venture capitalist and bank models are risk averse. This leads to only a small amount of startup ideas receiving funding. So we decided to make a site that allows anyone with an interest in investing, startups, business or a product to have the ability to invest in.

Our Goals

Our goal is to reinvigorate entrepreneurship and assist startups in changing the world! Wealth inequality is on the rise and entrepreneurship is decreasing across North America. We intend on countering these shifts by assisting businesses in raising funds. The end goal is to develop a startup stock market where equities can be traded amongst one another. This would allow us to fund thousands of companies that are riskier ventures that will grow into stable companies that will be the corner stones of a new and healthier economy.
