Individual Components

At Invstme innovators break down their business plans into individual components. This allows both transparency and lets investors pick what parts of the business they want to invest in.

Customize Your Investment

All campaigns on Invstme have different components that investors can put their money in. This system allows for different risk to reward ratios. 

Reduce Risk

Thanks to individual components, investors can customize their risk and return ratios to their comfort levels.

How Invstme Works

Invstme has two sides. One for investors and the other for entrepreneurs. 

Publish A Plan

Publish your business plan on Invstme and engage with other users on the site.

Review Funding Options

Just post how much funding you require, what you can offer an investor in return and any additional information.

Browse Ideas & Plans

Invstme offers investors access to numerous entrepreneurial ideas that provide both equity and other offers in return for funding.

Invest In Ideas You Believe In

Invstme gives you the freedom to invest your money in ideas you believe in. There is no maximum or minimum investing amount.

Raise Awareness

With Invstme, you can reach out to the world. Show everyone an idea and plan.

Collaborate & Contribute

Work with others who are part of the Invstme Community to achieve your goal.

Recieve Funds & Equity

Invstme allows everyone to raise funds for a project in return for equity or other components in a company.
