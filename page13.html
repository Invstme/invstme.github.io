Investment Opportunities Offered On Invstme

At the moment, Invstme offers investors the ability to support startups through six different types of investments or funding opportunities - Each with their own risk and reward profile.

Invstme is all about customizing your risk to reward ratio to fit your own investment strategy. Feel free to mix and match the various offerings below into something you're comfortable with! 

Equity

Equity in a company involves investors investing money in exchange for a percentage of the company.

Loans

Loans involve giving a company an amount of money for a certain amount of time or until cetain goals are met. Once these goals are met by the company or the amount of time has passed the company is required to pay back the loan with interest.

Bespoke Loan

A Bespoke Invstme Loan is similar to a regular loan. The difference is that a company must buy items that they need and the money that is invested will only be used to purchase these items. These loans are usually used to buy parts for products or other tangible items. When these items are sold the loan is paid back with interest. In the event of the startup failing, these items will be liquidated with the money generated from the liquidation being returned to the original investors of the bespoke loan.

Donation

A donation is usually a small amount of money that is given to the company in return for a small reward. Individuals who donate money to companies will not recieve any equity or their money back. With donations, individuals will usually give these companies moneey because they believe in them or their cause. Companies who recieve donations usually offer rewards or perks in return such as a hats, t-shirts, thank you letters or anything else they deem to be a reasonable means of saying thank you.

Buying The Product

Startups always have the opportunity to sell their finished product on the Invstme platform. This allows prospective investors the ability to try out the product before deciding whether they would like to invest in the company.

Custom

Startups have the ability to of offering a different type of investment should they decide. This custom investment will be vetted by the financial team at Invstme before being offered to the general public.
