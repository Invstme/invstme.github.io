Innovators & Investors

Both are integral components in the journey of a startup

Your Campaign, Your Way - Innovators

Innovation needs money for funding. Invstme allows you to leverage your company's assets in exchange for funds.

Leverage company assets for funds

Invstme allows campaigners to leverage their company's assets in exchange for funds. Intellectual property or product components can be an asset that is used as collateral and leveraged.  

Flexibility of terms

Invstme allows innovators to choose what terms they wish to give investors in return for funding. Whether it is equity in the company or profit sharing for a selected timeframe, Invstme gives you a choice in the decision.  

Your Money, Your Choice - Investors

Here at Invstme, we understand that investing involves risk. Some risks are worth it, others aren't. Only you can decide.

No minimum investment amounts.

Choose to invest as much or as little money as you want. Here at Invstme, there is no minimum amount of money necessary to become an investor.

Risk to Reward Ratios that fit your lifestyle

Every company has different risks attached to their business. Rather than just investing in a company as a whole, Invstme allows it's users to invest in different aspects of the business. Each with their own unique reward percentages based on their risk.  

Look at business plans from a variety of companies and choose to invest in their various aspects and components. Intellectual property, invest in it. Parts for a product, invest in that. Invstme gives you the freedom to finance what you believe in: your investment, your choice.
