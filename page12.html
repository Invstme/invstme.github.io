What is your goal? This section is where you would write where you see your company in the following 5 years. Write some milestones you intend to meet starting at 3 months, 6 months, 1 year, 2 years and 5 years following your funding round. This is also a good place to write about your exit strategy. Remember that investors are going to want to see a viable exit strategy as their returns on their investment is contingent on it. Some exit strategies include: being acquired by a larger company, an IPO to enter the stock market, a merger with another company or perhaps you intend on growing the company and paying dividend to your investors. Whatever the case may be, inform the investor in this section.     
FACTS ABOUT YOUR COMPANY - This section is a fantastic place to list key facts about your business. 
  • Goals you have reached.
  • Revenue
  • Sales
  • Profits
  • Patents or Intellectual Property or Trade Secrets 
  • This section is for you to list key pieces of information that you believe differentiate you from another startup or competitor.  It's okay to brag about your business in this section.
  • Highlight facts that you are proud of and what may have been missed by investors in the offering document

WHAT OUR FANTASTIC USERS SAY

This section should include testimonials from clients, users or beta testers. Let investors know that their money will be put to good use and show them that individuals who have experienced your product first hand enjoy it and believe in it. 
Also inclue any clippings or links your company has received from the press or blogs.
